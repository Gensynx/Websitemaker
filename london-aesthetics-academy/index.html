Meet Your Trainers

Taught by practising injectors who never stopped learning.

London's Aesthetics Academy was founded by Jodi & Levi — two practitioners with a genuine beauty and aesthetics background and over a decade of combined experience between them. We trained under some of the most respected names in the industry, and we still do: we attend refresher training constantly so that everything we know, and everything that's new, is passed directly to you.

We train both medics and non-medics, tailoring every course to your background — whether you're already established in beauty or starting completely from scratch. Alongside the academy we run private clinics, which means you learn from real, current practice, and you can even join us as a model on a training day before you enrol.

Why Train With Us

A boutique academy, not a conveyor belt.

We deliberately keep numbers low and standards high. You leave with real, repeatable skill — and a mentor for life.

Truly small groups

A maximum of four students per course means real one-to-one time, more hands-on practice and no getting lost in a crowd.

Live models, every time

You practise on real clients — never a mannequin or an orange. You'll perform genuine treatments before you ever qualify.

Accredited & insurable

Your certificate is CPD-accredited and recognised by leading insurers, so you can start treating clients legitimately from day one.

Lifetime mentorship

Training day doesn't end the relationship. Message us anytime, return for refreshers, and lean on us as your career grows.

Business & clinic support

We share how we built and run our own clinics — pricing, consent, marketing and confidence to launch your brand.

Medics & non-medics welcome

Whatever your starting point — established beauty pro or complete beginner — we tailor the pace and depth to you.
