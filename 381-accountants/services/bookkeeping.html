Bookkeeping

Accurate, up-to-date books are the foundation of every good business decision. We keep yours immaculate: daily transactions, reconciliations and clear monthly reports, all on cloud software you can check any time.

Book this service 020 8214 1259

Bookkeeping is the discipline that keeps everything else honest: your VAT returns, your year-end accounts and your view of how the business is really doing. Fall behind and every deadline becomes a scramble. Keep it current and the rest of your accounting takes care of itself.

Our bookkeepers record and categorise every transaction, chase down the missing receipts and reconcile your accounts so the numbers you look at are numbers you can trust. You get timely, readable reports instead of a shoebox of paperwork returned once a year.

What’s included

  • Daily transaction recording: every sale, purchase and expense captured and correctly categorised
  • Accounts payable & receivable: know exactly who you owe, who owes you, and when
  • Bank & credit card reconciliation: your books matched to your statements, line by line
  • Financial statements & reports: timely monthly reports that tell you how the business is performing
  • Cloud bookkeeping: Sage One, Sage 50 and QuickBooks, so you can access your numbers anywhere
  • Year-end ready records: books that flow straight into accounts and tax returns without the panic

How it works, step by step

  1. 1

    We review where you are

    A free consultation to look at your current records, software and volume of transactions. If there is a backlog, we size it honestly and include clearing it in your quote.

  2. 2

    We set up the system

    We connect or create your cloud accounts on Sage or QuickBooks, link your bank feeds and agree the simplest possible way for paperwork to reach us: app, email or a folder you drop things into.

  3. 3

    We keep the books, week in, week out

    Transactions are recorded and categorised on an agreed routine. We reconcile against your bank statements and chase anything that does not match before it becomes a mystery.

  4. 4

    You get the picture every month

    A clear monthly report shows income, spending, who owes you and how the business is tracking. Your books stay permanently year-end ready.

Worth knowing: Behind on your books? We regularly take on backlogs, sometimes months or years behind, and bring them fully up to date before switching you to a simple monthly routine.

Frequently asked

Which software do you work with?

We work with cloud-based accounting software including Sage One, Sage 50 and QuickBooks. If you are not on software yet, we will recommend the best fit for your business and set everything up for you.

Can you take over from our previous bookkeeper?

Yes. We handle the switch-over, review the state of your existing records, tidy anything that needs it and carry on without disruption to your business.

How often will my books be updated?

That depends on the volume of your transactions. Weekly or monthly suits most small businesses. We agree a routine with you and stick to it, so your reports are always current.
