Academy · CPD Course

Chemical Peel Course

From superficial refreshers to deeper resurfacing: acids, depths, skin types and the judgement to match them, taught hands-on.

IAbout this course

Chemical peels are deceptively simple: an acid, a brush, a timer. The craft is in everything around that, reading skin, choosing depth, managing expectations and knowing precisely when a peel is the wrong answer. That judgement is what this course teaches.

You will learn the chemistry without the chemistry degree: AHAs, BHAs and blended peels, what each depth genuinely achieves, and how skin type changes every decision, including when to decline.

Application technique is practised on live models in our W1 clinic under the direct supervision of a registered doctor, from preparation and timing to neutralising and aftercare.

IIWhat you will cover

Chemistry, judgement and hands, in that order.

  • Peel chemistry made usable: AHAs, BHAs, TCA and blends, without mystique
  • Depth and outcome: superficial to deeper peels, and what each honestly delivers
  • Skin assessment: Fitzpatrick types, contraindications and pre-peel preparation
  • Application technique: coverage, timing, layering and endpoints, practised live
  • Managing the peel journey: what clients experience day by day, and how to guide it
  • Complications and correction: pigmentation risks and how professionals prevent them

IIIHow your training runs

  1. Enquire and check suitability

    Tell us your background and goal. We confirm the right course and entry route for you honestly, including recommending a different starting point if that serves you better.

  2. Theory, properly taught

    Structured study of the science behind the procedure: anatomy, product knowledge, indications, contraindications and complication management, so your hands are guided by understanding.

  3. Supervised practice on live models

    Peel preparation, application, timing and neutralising practised on live models, with the supervising doctor guiding depth decisions case by case.

  4. Assessment and certification

    Your competence is assessed before you certify, and you leave with the LCA certificate for your course and a clear path for what to train in next.

IVAccreditation and recognition

CPD-accredited and taught by a registered clinic and training provider, with certification covering theory and supervised application.

Peels pair commercially with mesotherapy and skin boosters as a complete skin-quality menu, and progression routes exist to regulated qualifications.

VQuestions, answered

Is this course suitable for beginners?

Peels are an accessible entry into clinical skin treatments, and entry depends on background: beauticians, aestheticians and healthcare professionals each have a route. We confirm yours at enquiry.

Which peels will I be able to offer afterwards?

The course centres on the superficial-to-medium range where safe independent practice lives, with deeper work covered so you understand the full landscape and refer wisely.

How is darker skin covered?

Thoroughly; peel selection by skin type, pigmentation risk and preparation protocols are core modules, not asides. Treating all skin tones safely is non-negotiable.

Will I practise on live models?

Yes, in our working Marylebone clinic under direct doctor supervision.

How do I enrol?

Enquire through the contact page or email with the course name; we will confirm suitability and share dates personally.
