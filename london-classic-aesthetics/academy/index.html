The Training Academy

Train inside a working clinic.

Twelve courses, one standard: theory taught properly, then hands-on practice on live models under the direct supervision of a registered doctor, in the same Marylebone rooms where clients are treated.

Why train here

Supervision worth the name.

Plenty of academies rent a room and a mannequin. LCA trains you inside a registered, working clinic: live models, real client flow, and a registered doctor directly supervising your hands-on practice, correcting technique the moment it needs correcting.

  • Live models provided, so you practise on people, not props
  • Direct doctor supervision during every practical session
  • Recognised certification: CPD courses plus regulated ProQual, OFQUAL and QUALIFI routes
  • A certificate built to travel, designed for recognition across European countries

CPD Courses

Ten skills. One discipline.

Hands-on treatment courses, each pairing structured theory with supervised live-model practice. Start where your ambition points.

  • I Dermal Filler Foundation Your entry into injectables, supervised by a doctor
  • II Advanced Dermal Filler Cheeks, jaw, chin, tear trough and non-surgical rhinoplasty
  • III Skin Boosters The injectable hydration treatment clients ask for by name
  • IV PDO Threads Thread lifting: vectors, placement and judgement
  • V Mesotherapy Micro-injection therapy for skin quality and hair
  • VI Platelet-Rich Plasma Draw, spin, prepare, treat: the full regenerative workflow
  • VII IV Therapy Cannulation and infusion protocols for the wellness sector
  • VIII Phlebotomy The foundational clinical skill of drawing blood
  • IX Chemical Peels Acids, depths and the judgement to match them
  • X Anatomy & Physiology The theory underneath every safe treatment
