Academy · CPD Course

Platelet-Rich Plasma (PRP) Course

Train in regenerative treatment using the client’s own blood: draw, spin, prepare and reinject, taught end to end with clinical rigour.

IAbout this course

Platelet-rich plasma sits apart from other aesthetic treatments: nothing synthetic is injected at all. The client’s own blood is drawn, spun to concentrate the platelets, and returned to skin or scalp where those growth factors support repair and renewal.

Because PRP spans venepuncture, laboratory handling and injection technique, it is genuinely multi-skill training, and this course teaches every link in that chain rather than assuming any of them.

As always at LCA, the practical work happens on live models in our W1 clinic under the direct supervision of a registered doctor, from first draw to final injection.

IIWhat you will cover

Every stage of the PRP workflow, taught in order.

  • The science of PRP: platelets, growth factors and the honest evidence picture
  • Venepuncture: safe, confident blood draws (building on phlebotomy fundamentals)
  • Preparation: centrifugation, handling and maintaining a sterile chain
  • Facial application: injection and micro-needling-assisted techniques for skin quality
  • Scalp application: PRP for thinning hair, with realistic outcome-setting
  • Protocols and safety: screening, contraindications, aftercare and records

IIIHow your training runs

  1. Enquire and check suitability

    Tell us your background and goal. We confirm the right course and entry route for you honestly, including recommending a different starting point if that serves you better.

  2. Theory, properly taught

    Structured study of the science behind the procedure: anatomy, product knowledge, indications, contraindications and complication management, so your hands are guided by understanding.

  3. Supervised practice on live models

    The full PRP workflow practised on live models: draw, spin, prepare and treat, with the supervising doctor overseeing each stage.

  4. Assessment and certification

    Your competence is assessed before you certify, and you leave with the LCA certificate for your course and a clear path for what to train in next.

IVAccreditation and recognition

CPD-accredited training from a registered clinic and training provider, documenting the complete supervised workflow rather than the injection alone.

PRP pairs naturally with our Phlebotomy course for those newer to blood draws, and with mesotherapy for a complete regenerative skin-and-hair offering.

VQuestions, answered

Do I need to know how to take blood first?

It helps but is not assumed: venepuncture is taught within the course, and our dedicated Phlebotomy course exists if you want deeper grounding first. We will advise honestly at enquiry.

Is PRP evidence-based?

PRP has a genuine and growing evidence base, strongest in certain applications, and this course teaches you to represent that picture honestly to clients rather than oversell it.

What equipment will I learn on?

Clinical centrifuge and preparation systems of the kind used in practice, inside our working clinic, so nothing on your first solo day feels unfamiliar.

Face, hair, or both?

Both applications are covered, including how to select candidates for each and set expectations that survive contact with reality.

How do I secure a place?

Enquire via the contact page or email with the course name; suitability and dates are confirmed personally.
