Treatments · Skin & Hair

Mesotherapy

Vitamins, minerals and hyaluronic acid delivered as micro-injections exactly where skin and scalp can use them, because nutrition applied on top mostly stays on top.

IThe treatment

Mesotherapy is a series of very fine, very shallow injections that place a tailored cocktail of vitamins, minerals, amino acids and hyaluronic acid into the layer of skin where it can actually be used. The skin barrier that keeps serums out is simply stepped past.

On the face, mesotherapy is a treatment for quality rather than shape: hydration that lasts, a more even tone, the kind of glow that reads as sleep and good genes. On the scalp, targeted cocktails support weakening follicles, and it has a loyal following among clients noticing early thinning.

It is a gentle, cumulative treatment. Think of it as training for your skin: a planned course first, then maintenance at a rhythm that suits you.

IIWho it suits, honestly

Mesotherapy shines when the complaint is quality: tired, thirsty, lacklustre skin or early hair thinning. It does not lift, fill or freeze.

Consider it if

  • Dull, dehydrated skin that no serum seems to fix
  • Early fine lines from dryness rather than movement
  • Skin that looks tired even when you are not
  • Early hair thinning you want to act on sensibly
  • Preferring gradual, no-drama improvement

It is not for

  • Anyone under 18
  • Pregnancy or breastfeeding
  • Active infection or inflammation at the treatment site
  • Volume loss or deep folds, which are filler territory
  • Anyone expecting one session to transform anything

IIIYour appointment, step by step

Simple on the surface, precise underneath. Here is how a mesotherapy course at LCA actually runs.

  1. Consultation

    We look at your skin or scalp, talk through what you have tried, and design the right cocktail and course, or tell you honestly if a different treatment would serve you better.

  2. Preparation

    Cleansing and numbing cream. Mesotherapy needles are among the finest used in aesthetics, and most clients find the treatment far gentler than the word “injection” suggests.

  3. The session

    A pattern of micro-injections across the area. A session is unhurried but efficient, and you can usually return to your day straight after.

  4. Course and maintenance

    Results build over a planned course of sessions, then are maintained with occasional top-ups. We track progress photographs so improvement is measured, not imagined.

Safety, before style. Sterile single-use needles, pharmaceutical-grade cocktails, medical screening beforehand and a doctor-led setting throughout. Expect possible pinpoint marks or light flushing for a day or so, and clear written aftercare.

IVQuestions, answered

What is actually in the injections?

Blends of vitamins, minerals, amino acids and non-crosslinked hyaluronic acid, selected for your goal, hydration, radiance or hair support, and disclosed to you fully at consultation.

Is it painful?

It is remarkably tolerable. The needles are extremely fine and shallow, numbing cream is used, and most clients rate it easier than eyebrow threading.

When will I notice a difference?

Skin often looks fresher within days of a session, with the real, lasting change building across the course as hydration and quality improve cumulatively.

Mesotherapy or skin boosters?

They are cousins. Boosters place a larger depot of hyaluronic acid for deep hydration; mesotherapy delivers a broader nutrient mix more superficially. Many clients use both at different times, and we will recommend honestly.

Does it work for hair?

It supports follicles that are weakening, and works best started early and combined with a sensible overall plan. We will assess your scalp and be straight with you about what it can and cannot do.
