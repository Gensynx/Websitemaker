Anti-Wrinkle Injections

Botulinum toxin, prescribed by a doctor after examining your face, not sold as a product off a shelf. Softer lines, kept expression.

IThe treatment

Anti-wrinkle injections use botulinum toxin type A to relax the small muscles whose repeated movement etches lines into skin: the frown between the brows, the horizontal forehead lines, the crow’s feet that deepen with every smile. Used with restraint, it softens what is there and slows what is coming.

In the UK, botulinum toxin is a prescription-only medicine. That is not small print; it is the whole point of coming to a doctor-led clinic. At LCA you are assessed face to face, prescribed for individually, and treated with measured doses aimed at movement you keep, lines you lose.

Beyond the classic three areas, carefully placed toxin can lift a heavy brow tail, soften a gummy smile, ease dimpling on the chin and refine downturned mouth corners. Whether those suit you is exactly what the consultation is for.

IIWho it suits, honestly

This treatment rewards the right expectations. It works on lines caused by movement; it is not a filler, and it will not replace volume.

Consider it if

  • Frown or forehead lines that linger even when your face is at rest
  • Crow’s feet that make eye make-up sit badly
  • A brow that has begun to feel heavy
  • Preferring prevention in measured doses over correction later
  • Wanting a doctor’s assessment before your first ever treatment

It is not for

  • Anyone under 18 (UK law prohibits it)
  • Pregnancy or breastfeeding
  • Certain neuromuscular conditions, screened at consultation
  • Deep static folds better suited to fillers or skin treatments
  • Anyone wanting a completely frozen, expressionless look

IIIYour appointment, step by step

Because this is a prescription-only medicine, the process is deliberately unhurried. Expect two visits for a first treatment: consultation, then treatment.

  1. Consultation and prescription

    Dr Hina examines how your muscles move, discusses what bothers you, screens your medical history and, where appropriate, prescribes. You will know exactly what is being treated and why.

  2. Treatment

    A series of precise micro-injections with a very fine needle. The appointment itself is short, and most clients describe the sensation as brief pinpricks.

  3. The settling window

    Nothing happens immediately, and that is normal. The effect develops gradually over days, reaching its full result within about two weeks.

  4. Review and refine

    We invite you back to review the result once it has settled, and adjust if a small refinement would improve balance. Records are kept so your next visit starts from knowledge, not guesswork.

Safety, before style. Botulinum toxin has decades of clinical history and an excellent safety profile when prescribed and injected correctly. At LCA that means a doctor-led consultation, individual prescribing, sterile technique and honest dosing, never a one-size vial.

IVQuestions, answered

Will I still be able to make expressions?

Yes. The aim is relaxation, not paralysis. Doses are placed so you keep natural animation while the lines soften. A frozen look is a dosing choice, and it is not one we make.

How long does it last?

Typically a few months, with movement returning gradually rather than overnight. With regular measured treatments many clients find lines stay softer for longer.

When will I see the result?

The first change usually appears within days, with the full effect at around two weeks, which is when we review.

Is it safe to have before an event?

Plan backwards: allow at least two to three weeks so the result has fully settled and any tiny injection marks are long gone.

Why does the consultation matter so much?

Because botulinum toxin is a prescription-only medicine, and because faces differ. The consultation is where dose, placement and suitability are decided, and where you can ask anything at all.
