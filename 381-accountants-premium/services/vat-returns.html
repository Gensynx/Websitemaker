VAT Returns

VAT is one of the easiest taxes to get wrong and one of the costliest to get wrong repeatedly. We keep you registered, compliant and on the right scheme, with every return filed on time under Making Tax Digital.

Book this service 020 8214 1259

From the moment your turnover approaches the registration threshold, VAT becomes a quarterly obligation with real penalties attached. The rules change often, and the scheme you choose (standard, flat rate or cash accounting) can make a genuine difference to your cash flow.

Our team stays current with the latest VAT regulations so you do not have to. We prepare and file your returns from clean bookkeeping data, and we deal with HMRC if they ever have questions.

What’s included

  • VAT registration & deregistration: registered at the right time, deregistered when it no longer serves you
  • Scheme advice: standard, flat rate, cash accounting or annual: we pick what suits your business
  • Quarterly VAT returns: prepared from reconciled books and filed before the deadline, every quarter
  • Making Tax Digital: fully MTD-compliant filing through recognised software
  • EC sales & special cases: imports, exports, reverse charge and margin schemes handled correctly
  • HMRC correspondence: queries, inspections and repayments dealt with on your behalf

How it works, step by step

  1. 01

    We review your VAT position

    Are you on the right scheme? Should you be registered at all? We check your turnover, your sector and your cash flow, and put you on the footing that costs you least.

  2. 02

    We connect the data

    Your VAT returns are only as good as the books behind them, so we make sure the bookkeeping feeding them is reconciled and MTD-compliant in Sage or QuickBooks.

  3. 03

    We prepare each quarter

    Every quarter we compute the return, check the unusual items (imports, reverse charge, margin scheme sales) and send you a plain-English summary of what is due.

  4. 04

    We file and defend

    The return is filed digitally before the deadline. If HMRC ever queries or inspects, we handle the correspondence and paperwork from start to finish.

Worth knowing: On the wrong scheme, many businesses quietly overpay VAT for years. We review your position when we take you on; switching schemes has saved clients thousands.

Frequently asked

When do I have to register for VAT?

When your taxable turnover for the last 12 months passes the registration threshold, or you expect it to in the next 30 days. Register late and HMRC can backdate what you owe, so talk to us before you reach the line.

What is Making Tax Digital?

HMRC requires VAT records to be kept digitally and returns to be filed through compatible software. Our bookkeeping and filing are fully MTD-compliant, so you are covered automatically.

Can you take over a VAT dispute or inspection?

Yes. We act as your agent, prepare the paperwork HMRC asks for and manage the correspondence from start to finish.
