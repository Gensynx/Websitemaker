Payroll

Your people expect to be paid correctly and on time, and HMRC expects to hear about it the same day. We run payroll end to end, covering payslips, RTI submissions and pensions, so both stay happy.

Book this service 020 8214 1259

Payroll looks simple until you are the one running it: tax codes change, people start and leave mid-month, statutory pay rules shift, and every pay run has to be reported to HMRC in real time. Getting it wrong costs money and goodwill.

We run payroll for businesses of every size, from a single director to a growing team. You tell us the hours and any changes; we do everything else and send payslips ready to go out.

What’s included

  • PAYE scheme registration: we set you up as an employer with HMRC and keep the scheme compliant
  • Weekly or monthly pay runs: wages, overtime, bonuses, deductions and holiday pay calculated correctly
  • Payslips for every employee: clear, compliant payslips delivered each pay period
  • RTI submissions to HMRC: Full Payment Submissions filed on or before every payday
  • Pension auto-enrolment: assessment, enrolment and ongoing contributions handled for you
  • Starters, leavers & year end: P45s, P60s and end-of-year reporting all taken care of

How it works, step by step

  1. 01

    We register and set up

    If you are a new employer we register your PAYE scheme with HMRC. If you already run payroll, we collect the year-to-date figures and take over mid-year with no disruption.

  2. 02

    You send us the changes

    A couple of working days before payday, tell us hours, overtime, new starters or leavers. Fixed salaries with no changes run automatically without you lifting a finger.

  3. 03

    We run the payroll

    Pay, tax, National Insurance, pension contributions and any statutory pay are calculated, payslips are produced, and the RTI submission goes to HMRC on or before payday.

  4. 04

    We close the loops

    Pension contributions are submitted to your provider, P45s are issued when people leave, and P60s plus end-of-year reporting are handled every April.

Worth knowing: Directors’ payroll: running a limited company? We will set the most tax-efficient mix of salary and dividends alongside your payroll, so you keep more of what you earn.

Frequently asked

How much notice do you need for each pay run?

Send us hours and any changes a couple of working days before payday and everything will be ready on time. For fixed salaries with no changes, it runs automatically.

Can you handle pension auto-enrolment?

Yes: assessment of your workforce, letters to staff, enrolment with your pension provider and the ongoing contribution submissions each pay period.

We already run payroll. Is switching painful?

No. We take over mid-year regularly: we collect the year-to-date figures from your current provider or software and carry straight on.
