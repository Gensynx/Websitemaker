Our story

East London roots. At home in Canary Wharf.

381 Accountants was established in 2010 as an independent firm of certified accountants with a simple mission: to provide an efficient and proactive service to every client, and to assure them they are investing in the right accountants, regardless of size.

From our office at 30 Churchill Place in Canary Wharf we serve sole traders, landlords, contractors and limited companies across Canary Wharf, Barking, Ilford, Dagenham, Woodford, Bexley and the wider London area. Our senior team’s experience in the industry stretches back more than 25 years, through every change in tax law, filing regime and software along the way.

Clients come to us for a tax return or a tidy-up, and stay for years. Our Google reviews tell that story better than we can: 5.0 from 42 reviews, with reviewers describing relationships of five, ten and twenty-five years.

How we work

What “proactive” actually means here

Plenty of firms file what you send them. We think that is the smallest part of the job.

Ahead of deadlines

A person, not a portal

You get direct access to the people doing your work. Responsive, polite and efficient: their words, not ours.
