The house

Built on keeping our word

House of Autos started with one car, one phone number and a simple idea: hiring a serious car in London shouldn't feel like a gamble. No hidden fees at handover, no tired cars that don't match the photos, no silence when you have a question.

The collection has grown, the standard hasn't moved. Every hire still runs through the same hands, and every car is presented exactly as it appears on our page — because most of our bookings come from people who were sent here by someone we looked after.

House standards

How we run things

Prepared, not just cleaned

Every car is valeted inside and out, fuelled and checked before each hire. It leaves the house the way we would want to receive it.

Handover on record

A joint walk-round with photos at collection and return. No surprises on either side, and deposits released promptly after inspection.

One point of contact

You deal with the same person from first message to key return, over WhatsApp. Questions answered in minutes, not days.
